Brain Tumor Segmentation

This project is a brain tumor segmentation model based on the Brats 2020 dataset. The model uses a 3D Residual U-Net with skip connections to accurately segment brain tumors in medical images. By leveraging this architecture and dataset, the model achieves high accuracy and efficiency in detecting and segmenting brain tumors. This project has the potential to aid medical professionals in the diagnosis and treatment of brain tumors by providing accurate and efficient segmentation results.
